The Analytics

The Importance of Pricing

Properly priced, you will have the possibility of many more buyers competing for your property.

  • The Pyramid of Demand
    As you price above market value, you attract a significantly smaller percentage of prospective buyers. Pricing accurately at the centerline maximizes buyer pool penetration (attracting 60% or more of the total market).
  • The Interest Curve
    When a property is first listed, it generates a massive spike in buyer interest. This interest reduces dramatically over time. It is vital to be priced correctly from Day 1 to capitalize on the "Optimal Pricing Window."
