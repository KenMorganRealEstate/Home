The Residence

This nearly fully furnished beautiful corner unit is ideally located in a quiet cul-de-sac of private town homes. Close to restaurants, shopping, schools and parks. Open foyer with 2 piece bath and convenient inside garage entry. Bright open concept Great Room overlooks the huge eat in kitchen with lots of cupboard and counter space with beautiful stone backsplash. Garden door provides walkout to fully fenced private deck and stone patio. Master bedroom features a huge walk-in closet and oversized en-suite bathroom. In addition to 3 spacious bedrooms, the upper level also has laundry facilities, a full 4 piece bathroom and a large open office area! The unspoiled lower level is equipped with roughed in plumbing, ready to finish to individual taste. Roof re-shingled in 2017. Perfect for first time buyers or empty nesters!
