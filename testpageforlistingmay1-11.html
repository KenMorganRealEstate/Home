Listing Description

Welcome to 28 Celano Drive - A Stunning Park-Backed Family Home in the Heart of Waterdown! DUE TO STRONG INTEREST, PRICE ADJUSTED! Experience luxury, comfort, and an exceptional lifestyle in this beautifully upgraded and meticulously maintained 4 +1 bedroom, 4-bathroom home offering approximately 3,026 sq. ft. of finished living space. Built in 2017 and perfectly positioned backing directly onto Cranberry Park with private park access, this home offers rare privacy and scenic surroundings. Step inside to a bright and elegant open-concept main level featuring rich hardwood flooring, a designer kitchen with quartz countertops, upgraded cabinetry, premium KitchenAid stainless steel appliances, along with a brand-new LG 36" refrigerator and Samsung dishwasher. The spacious Great Room with a cozy fireplace creates the perfect setting for family gatherings, while a separate den provides an ideal space for a home office or study. The second level showcases new flooring throughout, a generously sized primary suite complete with a 3-piece ensuite and walk-in closet, along with three additional bedrooms, convenient second-floor laundry, and a large family room-perfect for relaxation or entertainment. The fully finished basement adds incredible value, featuring an additional bedroom, full bathroom, recreation area, and pantry-ideal for extended family, guests, or multi-generational living. This home is further enhanced with modern LED lighting throughout and elegant marble and stone finishes in the kitchen and all bathrooms, reflecting quality and attention to detail at every turn. Step outside to your private backyard oasis, fully fenced and featuring a spacious deck and an extra-large hot tub that doubles as a swimming pool-perfect for year-round enjoyment while backing onto the tranquility of Cranberry Park. Located in one of Waterdown's most sought-after neighborhood, you are just minutes from top-rated schools, shopping, restaurants, parks, and major highways in (42612860).
