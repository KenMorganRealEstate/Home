Run your business, manage your finances, and track your personal goals.

Checklist

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

SOP: Daily CEO Routine

This is the daily non-negotiable routine for running your business.

  1. Morning: Open the **CEO Scorecard**. Review the "12-Month Plan" to orient on long-term goals.
  2. Throughout Day: As you complete them, check off the 5 "Daily CEO Scorecard" habits: `#1 Business Priority`, `Weight Training`, `5km Walk`, `On-Plan Nutrition`, `No Alcohol`.
  3. After Travel: After *any* business-related trip (showings, appointments), immediately open the **Business Travel Log** and log the `Date`, `Description`, `Purpose`, `Starting Odometer`, and `Ending Odometer`.
  4. End of Day:
    • - Log your `Current Weight` and any `Daily Notes` in the **CEO Scorecard**.
    • - Open the **Real Estate Performance Dashboard** to log any new events (e.g., `Daily Contacts`) or expenses (`General Expense`).
SOP: Weekly Financial Review

Once a week (e.g., Friday afternoon), perform a full financial sync.

  1. Download the latest CSV statements from your bank and credit card.
  2. Use the **CSV File Reader** to quickly check the format and headers.
  3. Open the **Real Estate Performance Dashboard**. Click "Import CSV" and import your business expense CSV. The tool will parse the file, use your saved `categorizationRules` to auto-categorize, and flag any new descriptions for review.
  4. Open the **Vacation/Personal Expense Tracker** and import your personal CSVs to keep finances separate.
  5. In the **Performance Dashboard**, review the "Key Performance Indicators" (KPIs) and charts. Pay close attention to:
    • - Net Income
    • - Gross Income vs. Total Expenses
    • - Income / Contact (to see if your lead gen is efficient)

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

SOP: New Lead Entry

This is the first and most critical step for any new contact.

  1. Open the **Real Estate Business Hub (CRM)**.
  2. Click **"New Contact"**.
  3. Fill in all known details: `Full Name`, `Contact Status` (set to "New Lead"), `Email`, `Phone`, `Source` (e.g., "Open House", "Door Knocking", "Referral").
  4. **CRITICAL STEP:** In the **"Apply Action Plan"** dropdown, select the nurture sequence that matches the source.
    • - **Open House:** "Open House Lead (High Intent)"
    • - **Door Knock:** "Door Knocking (Cold to Warm)"
    • - **Friend/Past Client:** "Sphere of Influence"
    • - **Other:** "Generic Encounter"
  5. Set the `Plan Start Date` to today and click **"Save Contact"**.
  6. The system will now automatically schedule all follow-up tasks for you.
SOP: Daily CRM Task Execution

Your "Daily Contacts" are now automated. Simply follow the "To-Do" list.

  1. Open the **Real Estate Business Hub (CRM)**. The "CRM Dashboard" tab shows your tasks for today.
  2. Find a task (e.g., "Day 1: Crucial Follow-up Call" for [Client]).
  3. Click the **"Do Task"** button.
  4. The **Script Viewer** will pop up with the exact, pre-written script for that task.
  5. Use the "Send Email" or "Send Text" buttons to execute the follow-up.
  6. Click **"Mark as Complete"**. This logs the activity to the client's file and removes it from your to-do list.
SOP: Open House Lead Capture

Use the Financing Toolkit as your primary lead capture mechanism instead of a simple sign-in sheet.

  1. Have the **Home Financing Toolkit** open on your laptop or tablet.
  2. Greet visitors and offer them immediate value: "Welcome! While you look around, feel free to use my financing toolkit to see what the payments would look like."
  3. Walk them through the **"Affordability Calculator"** to pre-qualify them on the spot.
  4. Use the **"Closing Cost Estimator"** (toggle "First-Time Home Buyer") to educate them.
  5. This process naturally builds authority and gives you their real name, email, and financial details to enter directly into the **CRM**.

SOP: Pre-Appointment Checklist (from Brain Dump)
  1. Pre-research the area for [Property] and have an idea of sales and actives.

  3. Ensure all decision-makers will be present.

SOP: Generating the AI-Powered CMA (The "Deep Data" Workflow)

This is the "recipe" for creating your core presentation asset. It uses two AI tools in combination.

Step 1: Get Market & ROI Data
  1. Open the **Real Estate Improvement Analyzer** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1HykQcFIqQ3-TUbDvma584FDIiNATYHl0?showPreview=true&showAssistant=true`).
  2. Enter the `LOCATION` (e.g., "Burlington, ON") and `PROPERTY_TYPE` (e.g., "Detached Bungalow").
  3. Run the analysis. The AI will search the web for real-time data.
  4. **Copy the entire output** (the "Market Trends" summary and the "Improvements" list).
Step 2: Get Comparables Data
  1. Log in to the MLS (ITSO: `HBMORGAKE` / `ilovemoney11`).
  2. Pull all relevant "Sold" and "Active" comparable properties.
  3. Export this data as a single **PDF** file.
  4. Open the PDF, select all text (Ctrl+A), and **copy** it (Ctrl+C).
Step 3: Generate the Unified Valuation
  1. Open the **Deep Data CMA & Marketer** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1jvfta3v85lj5HKepXQlikvslq-IDsf-g?showPreview=true&showAssistant=true`).
  2. In the `Subject Property Address` field, enter the client's address: [Property].
  3. In the `Subject Property Details` field, enter your "brain dump" of notes (e.g., "3 bed, 2 bath, approx 1500 sq ft, original kitchen, new roof 2020, unfinished basement").
  4. In the `Comparables Text` field, **paste** the text from the comparables PDF.
  5. In the `Market Conditions` field, **paste** the full market/ROI analysis you copied from the *Improvement Analyzer*.
  6. In the `Listing Blueprint` field, paste your master listing presentation script (from Phase 9).
  7. Click **"Generate Unified Valuation."** The AI will generate your two key reports:
    • **Seller Report:** The full CMA, ROI analysis, and Marketing Toolkit. This is what you will present.
    • **Buyer Report:** The "Anticipated Offer Strategy." This is your private negotiating guide.
  8. Click **"Save to Library"** in the app to save this full report.
SOP: Preparing Paperwork
  1. Open the **CASORA Transaction Guide** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1TvnIwiI5orI7-e02cg_jEAM7wmaNb11u?showAssistant=true&showPreview=true`).
  2. Select "Lead Prep" -> "Seller." The tool will display the list of all required forms.
  3. Go to your forms provider (e.g., WebForms) and prepare these documents for digital signature.
  4. **Key Forms:** Listing Agreement (Form 200), FINTRAC Individual ID Record, Working with a Realtor (Form 810), MLS Data Input Sheet.

SOP: Building Rapport & The Tour

Follow the "Brain Dump" script precisely.

What to Say:

On Arrival:

"As I drove up, I couldn’t help but notice that your house sticks out from all the rest. The work you’ve done on the [landscaping/front door] is really great and will be really attractive to the next potential buyer."

Start the Tour:

"Mind if I set my things down at the table? Why don't you give me the grand tour, and I’ll ask some questions as we go so I can get to know the home a little bit better."

During the Tour:

"How long have you lived here? Where did you move from?"
"How did you find this home?"
"What's their favourite part of the home (why they chose)?" (Make a note of this)
SOP: Presenting the AI-Generated CMA
What to Say:

1. Set the Stage (Mike Ferry Script):

"At the end of my presentation tonight, one of three things will happen: 1. You’ll have the opportunity to list your home with me... 2. You’ll decide not to list your home with me... or 3. I’ll decide not to take your listing… any one is fine."

2. Confirm Motivation:

"Let’s quickly review what we discussed. You said you’re moving to [Their Destination]... and you had to be there by [Their Date]... correct? Great. Now, there are only two issues we have to look at tonight: number one, your motivation to sell this home, and number two, the price we set on your home. Are we clear on that?"

3. Introduce the CMA:

"I’ve prepared what we call a Comparative Market Analysis. There are two parts to this: Part one we call 'Fantasy Land'—what homeowners *list* homes for. Part two we call 'Reality'—what real estate agents *list and sell* homes for. We’re going to have to decide tonight where you’re going to spend your time."
What to Do:
  1. Open the **Deep Data CMA & Marketer** tool on your laptop/tablet.
  2. Load the saved valuation you prepared for [Client].
  3. Select the **"Seller"** perspective tab.
  4. Present the **CMA Report** section, focusing on the data-driven adjustment grid and the final **Estimated Value Range**.
  5. Present the **Improvement ROI** section to show them how your analysis is based on real-time data.
  6. Present the **Marketing Toolkit** to demonstrate your comprehensive plan.

SOP: Initial Buyer Consultation

This is the "Buyer's Presentation" and qualification process.

  1. Meet with the buyer ([Client]). Open the **Home Financing Toolkit** (`https://kenmorganrealestate.github.io/Home/7PerfectedOpenHouseMortgagePrintoutToolBrandedTabbedCalculators.html`) on your laptop.

  3. **Step 2 (Costs):** Use the **"Closing Cost Estimator"** tab. Be sure to toggle "First-Time Home Buyer" and "Municipality (e.g., Toronto)" to show them the full cash requirement.
  4. **Step 3 (Payments):** Use the **"Payment Calculator"** tab to run scenarios on different purchase prices, showing them the breakdown of P&I, Taxes, and Fees.
  5. **Step 4 (Investment):** Use the **"Long-Term Investment Projection"** (at the bottom of the Payment Calculator) to demonstrate the wealth-building advantages.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

SOP: Create Offer Strategy (AI Task)

Use this AI workflow to build a data-driven offer *before* you open your forms software.

  1. Gather documents: The Subject Property MLS Sheet, and 3-5 MLS Sheets for Comparable Properties.
  2. Open **REALegal Assist** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1mHgLRHS0MYgln8JCPde64y_f_o_JXkRp?showPreview=true&showAssistant=true`).
  3. Select the **"Architect Offer"** module.
  4. **Upload:** Upload the *Subject Property MLS Sheet* and all *Comparable Property MLS Sheets*.
  5. **Instructions:** Enter your buyer's goals (e.g., "We need a 60-day closing. Clients are pre-approved. They like the house but feel the kitchen is old. Be safe with conditions.").
  6. **Run the tool.** It will generate a full strategy, including:
    • - A justified price recommendation based on the comps.
    • - A list of all essential clauses (Financing, Inspection, Survey, etc.).
    • - Creative suggestions (e.g., "Consider asking for patio furniture").
  7. **Copy** the recommended clauses to your clipboard.
SOP: Draft & Verify Offer Paperwork (AI Task)
  1. Open the **CASORA Transaction Guide** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1TvnIwiI5orI7-e02cg_jEAM7wmaNb11u?showAssistant=true&showPreview=true`).
  2. Select **"Guided Appointment"** -> **"Offer Execution"** phase.
  3. Use the on-screen prompts for **"Agreement of Purchase & Sale"** as a checklist to fill in your OREA Form 100.
  4. When you get to Schedule A, **paste** the clauses you copied from **REALegal Assist**.
  5. If you need more clauses, use the **"Find Clauses"** module in **REALegal Assist** (e.g., "clause for septic").
  6. Use the `CASORA Guide` to complete Form 801 (Offer Summary) and any other disclosure forms.
  7. **Final Check:** Before sending, use the **"Analyze Documents"** module in **REALegal Assist**. Upload all your completed forms (APS, 801, etc.). The AI will cross-reference them and flag errors (e.g., "Warning: Buyer's name is spelled 'John Smith' on Form 100 but 'Jon Smith' on Form 801.").
SOP: Offer Presentation & Negotiation (from Brain Dump)
Offer Submission:
  1. Let one day go by after the showing to ask for feedback.
  2. Register the offer with the listing brokerage. Get a presentation time.
  3. Call your clients and advise them of the time.
  4. Advise all other agents who have shown the property of the offer. "Let me know if you can get your clients 'on paper'."
  5. Call the first agent back and tell them if there is competition. "You’re in competition – tell your purchasers."
Presenting the Offer (as Listing Agent):
  1. Mentally prepare the vendor.
  2. Have the agent present their Offer (and Form 801). Distribute copies to all at the table.
  3. "Let’s all read over this (silently) first. Once everybody’s read it we can open it up for discussion."
  4. Read the offer aloud, every word. Check spelling, legal description, conditions, and closing date (ensure not on a weekend/holiday).
  5. Thank the agent and dismiss them.
  6. **If you are the Listing Agent**, this is when you open your pre-generated **"Anticipated Offer Strategy"** from the **Deep Data CMA** tool to advise your sellers.

SOP: Verifying Waivers & Amendments (AI Task)

Never accept a waiver without verifying it.

  1. When a "Notice of Fulfillment" or "Waiver" is received, open **REALegal Assist** (`https://aistudio.google.com/app/apps/drive/1mHgLRHS0MYgln8JCPde64y_f_o_JXkRp?showPreview=true&showAssistant=true`).
  2. Select the **"Analyze Documents"** module.
  3. Upload the new document (e.g., `Waiver.pdf`) AND the original `Agreement of Purchase and Sale.pdf`.
  4. The AI will check for errors, such as:
    • - Mismatched dates or addresses.
    • - Confirming the condition being waived matches a condition in the original APS.
    • - Ensuring all required signatures and initials are present.
  5. Once verified, circulate the firm deal to all parties.

Script: Powerful Closes for Listing Presentations

Close 1 (The Assumptive Close):

"To give your property maximum exposure... I am going to recommend we get a lockbox on the property right away... a sign in the front yard... and of course... we’ll get it on the internet as fast as possible... Now... when would you like us to come by with the sign and lockbox? Sign the contract."

Close 2 (The Simple Close):

"Do you feel I can sell your home? Sign the contract."

Close 3 (The Eliminator Close):

"Is there anything else stopping you from listing your home with me tonight? Sign the contract."

Close 4 (The Agent Canceler Close):

"As a professional service... and to start our relationship... I’ll call the agents you still have appointments with... and cancel the appointments for you... Would you like to know what I’m going to say? ... 'They thought you were terrific... They wanted to give you a chance but... I convinced them to... list with me tonight!' ... Let’s make a list of who I need to call."

Close 5 (The Direct Close):

"Mr. & Mrs. Seller... Based on your time frame... what you want for your home... and the marketing and exposure you need... I’m the right person for the job... Put me to work for you right now... Sign the contract."

Close 6 (The Doctor Analogy):

"Have you noticed that all Realtors say basically the same thing? Let me show you how I separate myself... I sell [X] homes per year while my competitors average [Y] per year... If you were going to hire a doctor to perform surgery... would you hire someone who does it now and then... or... the doctor who’s done it day in and day out for years? ... I agree! Go ahead and sign the contract."

Close 7 (The Final Question):

"When would you like to receive your first offer? ... All we need to do now is simply... sign the contract... so I can help you get what you want in the time you want... won’t that be great?"
